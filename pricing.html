Questions

Pricing, plainly answered.

Can I change plans or cancel anytime?
Yes. Upgrade, downgrade, or cancel whenever you want — no contracts and no cancellation fees. If you downgrade, your projects and captures stay intact; the features specific to the higher plan simply turn off until you upgrade again.
How does billing work on iPhone?
FieldBinder AI is an iPhone-first app, so paid subscriptions are handled through your Apple App Store account as an in-app purchase. That means one place to manage or cancel — right in your iPhone settings — and your existing Apple payment method on file.
What counts against my storage?
Photos, short videos, voice notes, and uploaded files. Text notes, tags, and reports are negligible. Short videos are capped at 30–60 seconds specifically to keep storage predictable — so a typical project stays light even with heavy photo documentation.
Is my field data used to train AI models?
Your project data lives in your own private workspace and is used to generate your reports — not shared with other users. FieldBinder AI is a documentation and drafting tool built around keeping your project memory organized and secure. Full details will be spelled out in the Privacy policy.
Are the AI reports a substitute for professional judgment?
No. AI-generated reports are editable drafts — a fast first pass built from what you captured. You're responsible for reviewing every report for accuracy and completeness before sharing it. FieldBinder AI is a drafting assistant, not a licensed engineering, legal, safety, or compliance authority.
When will the Team plan be available?
Team — shared projects, company workspaces, multi-user access, and admin controls — is on the roadmap after the individual plans are established. Join early access and we'll notify you the moment it opens, along with launch pricing.
