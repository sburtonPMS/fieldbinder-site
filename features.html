01 · Field capture

Everything you document on site, in one project.

Twelve capture types built around how field work actually happens — not a notes app bolted onto a photo library. Open the project, tap capture, and it lands in the right place with a timestamp and location.

  • Photos, short videos, voice notes, and text notes
  • Measurements, files, to-dos, and punch items
  • Customer decisions, safety observations, change requests, RFIs

02 · Project organization

Sorted automatically, the moment you capture.

Every project has a chronological feed as its home. Tag items as you go — one tap — and filter the whole job by type, tag, or date. No more scrolling a camera roll trying to remember which photo went with which issue.

  • Chronological project feed as the main view
  • Default tags: Scope, Issue, Progress, Punch, Safety, Change, RFI, and more
  • Filter by capture type, tag, or date instantly

03 · AI reporting engine

The part that saves the real time.

Talk while you walk and get a clean field note back. Let AI describe your photos. Then generate a full report from what you captured — daily report, punch list, RFI list, or executive summary — as an editable draft you review and export.

  • Voice-to-clean-note: rough dictation becomes a professional field note
  • AI photo descriptions you can accept or edit
  • Four report types generated from real field captures
Raw voice memo
"North wall has a damaged plate. Need contractor to verify existing steel. Customer wants light, medium, heavy repair options."
AI field note
Damage was observed along the north wall plate area. Contractor should field-verify existing steel conditions prior to repair. Recommend developing light, medium, and heavy repair options for owner review.
04 · Offline mode

Works where the signal doesn't.

Plants, basements, rooftops, data centers, remote job sites — the places you actually work rarely have bars. FieldBinder AI captures everything locally and syncs automatically the moment you're back online. Nothing waits, nothing gets lost.

  • Full offline capture — photos, video, voice, and notes
  • Clear pending-sync status on every item
  • Automatic sync when the connection returns
