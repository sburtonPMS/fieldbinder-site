The problem

Your project information is everywhere.

Photos are in the camera roll. Notes are in the phone. Videos are buried in text threads. Follow-ups live in someone's head.

Then it all has to become a report — a daily log, a punch list, an RFI, a customer update, a summary for leadership. So every site visit ends the same way: hours of after-hours cleanup, sorting a camera roll and reconstructing what happened from memory.

That admin drag is billable time you're giving away, and the project details lose context the moment you leave site.

How it works

Capture once. Let the project remember.

FieldBinder AI keeps the project memory for you — organized by job, ready to turn into a report whenever you need one.

Flagship feature

Talk while you walk. Get a clean field note.

Record a rough voice memo on site — the way you'd actually say it. FieldBinder AI transcribes it and rewrites it into a clear, professional field note you'd be comfortable putting in front of an owner. No typing with gloves on. No cleanup back at the truck.

Raw voice memo
"North wall has a damaged plate. Need contractor to verify existing steel. Customer wants options for light, medium, heavy repair."
AI field note
Damage was observed along the north wall plate area. Contractor should field-verify existing steel conditions prior to repair. Recommend developing light, medium, and heavy repair options for owner review.
